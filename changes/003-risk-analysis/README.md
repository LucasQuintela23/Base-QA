# 003 - Risk Analysis

## Goal
Map business and technical risks with mitigation strategies.

## Matrix Format
Risk | Category | Probability (H/M/L) | Impact (H/M/L) | Mitigation

## Checklist
- [ ] High impact risks are prioritized.
- [ ] Mitigation is actionable and testable.
- [ ] Risk owner is defined.
